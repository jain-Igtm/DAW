How to use it

Start the mic, sing a clean sustained note, and watch the needle. Center means in tune. Left is flat, right is sharp. Record WAV captures uncompressed PCM from the microphone so you can download a take and move it into BandLab or another editor.

This uses browser microphone access, so it needs HTTPS. GitHub Pages gives that automatically once Pages is enabled for this repo.

offline vocal correction

Retune a solo vocal WAV

Upload a dry vocal, choose the key, set how hard it should snap, then preview and download a corrected WAV. This works best on one voice at a time, not vocals mixed with a beat.
